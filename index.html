Signs of Healthy Nails

  • Nail plates are pinkish-white in color
  • Cuticles are existent, so don't cut them
  • Prescence of prominent half-moon shaped white section(referred to as lunula) at the nail base

    • Signs of Unhealthy Nails

  • Peeling or splitting nails could be because of dehydration or vitamin deficiency
  • Tiny white spots(which means you bite your nails or paint them too often)
  • Red, swollen skin around nails could be from biting off cuticles
  • Spoon shaped nails could be a sign of iron deficiency(anaemia)
  • Changes in nail shape

    • Best Nail Care Tips

  • Wash your hands regularly
  • Always keep your finger nails dry and clean
  • Use hand or nail moisturizer or nail oils as often as possible
  • Trim your nails regularly
  • Tame your cuticles
  • Stop biting your nails
  • Never scrape nail polish off your nails
  • If you must file your nails, file them in one direction
  • Avoid harsh polishes
  • Use natural nail-strengthening treatment
  • Prioritize nail health over length
  • Sterilize or disinfect your nail cutter and other nail care tools
  • Wear hand gloves when doing dirty work
  • Exercise caution when getting manicure
  • Eat more of protein-rich food
  • Drink a lot of water
  • Maintain good personal hygiene
